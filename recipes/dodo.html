Fried plantain, a delicious snack or meal.

Fried plantains is a nice and easy snack to prepare. It can also be fn too :)

The ingredients you'll need are;

  • Plantains
  • Salt
  • Spices (optional)
  • Groundnut oil/li>

Procedure to prepare this dish

  1. Cut plantains into preferred shape and size
  2. Salt plantains and add spices if you want to
  3. Fry till it turns golden brown
  4. Serve and eat
