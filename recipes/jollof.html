Jollof Rice: A local delicacy

Jollof rice is a go to dish and a personal favorite. It is easy to make and very delicious.

The ingredients you'll need are;/p>

  • Tomato (Fresh and paste)
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Any protein of your choice
  • Washed rice
  • Groundnut oil

SProcedure to prepare this dish

  1. Saute onions
  2. Add tomatoes into hot oil
  3. Fry for about 8 minutes
  4. Add the spices to the paste
  5. Allow to cook for 3 extra minutes
  6. Add stock from the cooked protein to the paste
  7. Add washed rice and allow to cook
  8. Serve
