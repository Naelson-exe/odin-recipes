Noodles: A personal favorite

Noodles are a very tasty and cost effective meal. It pairs nicely with a lot of things.

The ingredients you'll need are;/p>

  • Two packets of instant noodles
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Three eggs
  • Veggies of your choice
  • Groundnut oil

SProcedure to prepare this dish

  1. Saute onions
  2. Add water to the oil
  3. Add spices to the water
  4. Add the noodles to the water
  5. Allow to cook for 3 extra minutes
  6. Add diced veggies to the noodles and stir well
  7. Cook eggs
  8. Serve
